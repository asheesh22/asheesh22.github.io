Doctorate Thesis


Title Experimental and model-based analysis of twin-screw wet granulation in pharmaceutical processes

Supervisors Prof Ingmar Nopens (UGent), Prof. Thomas De Beer (UGent) & Prof. Krist V Gernaey (DTU)

Description The switch from batch to continuous granulation is a necessary step for initialising continuous production and improving both quality and economics of pharmaceuticals manufacturing. However, this adaptation is hampered by insufficient knowledge on the effect that process and machinery variables can have on the final granules properties. Computational modelling tools based on mechanistic and fundamental laws are very useful to gain insight into this complex problems and can help to achieve the target. In this project such modelling tools were caliberated and validated using experimental data, and applied for development of the detailed process knowledge.
